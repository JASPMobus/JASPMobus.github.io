James Mobus graduated the University of Dallas with a BS in Mathematics, but was intrigued by the programming electives he took. He decided to pursue that interest and take a course in Software Engineering at the Flatiron School to transition into computer science, using the logical intuition and problem solving skills he learned in his math schooling to tell computers how to solve problems.
